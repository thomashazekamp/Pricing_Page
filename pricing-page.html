Flex E-Pen Pricing and Retail Information

The Flex E-Pen has many features which can be added by selecting the more advanced options below. The big selling point for this E-pen is that it can write on multiple surfaces and if chosen it can also record conversations or lecture with the Pro Plus version of the Flex E-Pen. Every edition of the Flex E-Pen comes with a free mobile app to control and select the perfect writing style for you.

Flex E-Pen Standard Edition

This is the most basic edition of the Flex E-Pen and is great for a person who only need a simple yet useful pen that can write on multiple surfaces. In the box itself you will find the pen, a pen case to transport and protect the pen and there is the option to choose between black or white colour pen.

Order Now

Flex E-Pen Pro Edition

This edition of the Flex E-Pen is by far the most popular. It includes all of the accessories of the Standard edition and more, such as waterproofing (maximum depth of 30cm and up to 1 hour) and the option to engrave your name.

Order Now

Flex E-Pen Pro Plus Edition

The most advanced edition of the Flex E-Pen is the Pro Plus which gives the same features as the two above editions and it has a recording device built-in at the top of the pen. The recordings can be accessed through the mobile app. Through the mobile app you can export the audio files and even edit them to your liking.
